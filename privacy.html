Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Whaley Inc ("Whaley Inc," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you request or receive our vehicle escort and transport services, how we use it, and the choices you have — including with respect to text message (SMS) communications.

Information We Collect

When you submit a service request through our website or contact us by phone, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number
  • Vehicle information (year, make, model)
  • Service location and scheduling details
  • Payment method preference (we do not store full payment card numbers)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to coordinate and deliver the service you requested. This includes:

  • Confirming your booking
  • Sending status updates (e.g. driver dispatched, pickup, delivery, or job completion)
  • Contacting you with questions related to your specific request

SMS / Text Messaging

By submitting a service request through our website and checking the consent box, you agree to receive text messages and calls from Whaley Inc at the phone number you provide, regarding your booking, dispatch, and delivery status.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your service activity.

To opt out of text messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us. For help, reply HELP or contact us directly at (310) 748-0040.

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Your information is used only to deliver the service you requested.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited booking details (such as pickup/delivery location and vehicle information) with drivers or subcontractors directly involved in fulfilling your service request. We do not share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain booking and contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill your service request and to meet recordkeeping and legal obligations.

Your Choices

  • You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP.
  • You may request that we delete your contact information by emailing info@whaleyinc.net.
