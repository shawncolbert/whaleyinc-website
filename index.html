Before Pickup

Prepare Your Vehicle

A few simple steps make pickup and delivery faster and help avoid disputes over condition.

✓

Remove personal items. Belongings left inside aren't covered and can add weight or shift during transport.

✓

Keep the tank around a quarter full. This keeps the vehicle light and is plenty of fuel for loading and unloading.

✓

Document existing condition. Take photos of all sides, the roof, interior, and odometer before pickup.

✓

Disable toll tags & alarms. Prevents unexpected charges or alerts during transport.

✓

Have someone available at pickup & delivery. You or an authorized designee must be present to sign off — let us know in advance if that's not possible.

✓

Non-operating vehicle? Let us know when booking — our driver will bring the right equipment for loading.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be there for pickup and delivery?

+

Yes — you or an authorized designee must be present to inspect and sign off on the vehicle at both pickup and delivery. If you can't be there, let us know in advance and we'll coordinate with whoever you designate.

Can you transport a vehicle that doesn't run?

+

Yes. Let us know when booking that the vehicle is non-operational — our driver will bring the right equipment (winch, dolly, etc.) to load it safely. A surcharge applies; see our rate calculator above.

When do I pay?

+

You won't be charged until your pickup or delivery is confirmed and scheduled. We accept Zelle, Cash App, Venmo, Apple Pay, and all major credit cards.

Will the price change after I'm quoted?

+

No. Once your driver is dispatched, the price you were quoted is the price you pay. The only exception is if surcharge conditions (non-operating, oversized, etc.) weren't disclosed at booking — we'll always confirm those with you by phone before dispatch.

Who actually drives my vehicle?

+

Whaley Inc dispatches your load to a T-W-I-C certified, independently licensed driver from our vetted network. Every driver carries their own credentials and is familiar with port gate procedures at Long Beach, Wilmington, and Matson.

How far in advance should I book?

+

For standard pickups we recommend at least 24–48 hours notice when possible. We also handle same-day hotshot requests — call us directly for urgent jobs.

What areas do you cover?

+

We serve the Port of Long Beach, Port of Wilmington, and Port of Matson, with delivery throughout Greater Los Angeles, Orange County, the Inland Empire, San Diego, Northern California, and 48-state delivery available on request.
